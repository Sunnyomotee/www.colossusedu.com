Grow your skills to transition your Career into Tech

Acquiring tech skills in today's digital era is essential across all professions as technology permeates every industry, offering opportunities for enhanced employability and productivity through skills like digital marketing, web development, data analysis, and cybersecurity.

Globally, technology serves as the bedrock of national transformation, particularly in regions like Africa, highlighting the need for investment in professional training to meet the ever-increasing demand for digital skills worldwide. At Colossus Education, our seasoned instructors possess expertise in various IT fields, making us well-equipped to bridge the IT skills gap. Join us in acquiring essential tech skills for success in the digital age.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the right courses for me?

Fill the registration form honestly and take the interview with one of our course advisors.

What is the duration of the courses?

Each course is for an 8-week cycle.

Will I be given a certificate at the end of the programme?

Yes, all students that completes the programme, finishes the project and passes our final assesment will be eligible for a certificate.

Are there guranteed job placements available?

All graduating students are entitled to internships with our subsidiaries and best students are recommended for remote international job placements.

What is the mode of physical classes?

The physical classes are gonna be held in 2 batches: The weekday students taking their classes on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays (10am - 4pm) while the weekend students take their classes on Saturdays (10am - 5pm) and one virtual classes.

Are our trainers professionally qualified?

All our Instructors are professionally qualified and well versed in their respective fields.
